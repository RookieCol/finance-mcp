# NNNN — Title (a decision, phrased as a statement)

**Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Superseded by NNNN · **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What forces are at play — the problem, the constraints, what prompted deciding now. Two or three sentences.

## Decision

What we chose, stated plainly. Include the rejected alternative(s) in one line each if they were seriously considered.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what we've committed to maintaining. Include the trade-off we knowingly accepted.
